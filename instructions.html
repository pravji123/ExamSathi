Instructions

  1. The clock will be set at the server. The countdown timer at the top right corner of screen will display the remaining time available for you to complete the examination.
  2. When the timer reaches zero, the examination will end by itself. You need not terminate the examination or submit your paper.
  3. The Question Palette displayed on the right side of screen will show the status of each question using one of the following symbols:
    You have not visited the question yet.
    You have not answered the question.
    You have answered the question.
    You have NOT answered the question, but have marked the question for review.
    You have answered the question, but marked it for review.
  4. The Mark For Review status for a question simply indicates that you would like to look at that question again. If a question is answered, but marked for review, then the answer will be considered for evaluation unless modified.
  5. Navigating to a Question:
    1. Click on the question number in the Question Palette to go to that question directly (does NOT save your current response).
    2. Click Save & Next to save your answer and move to the next question.
    3. Click Mark for Review & Next to save and mark the question for review.
  6. You can view all questions by clicking on the Question Paper button. This feature helps you glance through the paper quickly.

    7. Answering a Question

    1. Procedure for answering a Multiple Choice Question (MCQ):
      • Choose one answer from the 4 options (A, B, C, D) given below the question.
      • Click on the bubble placed before the chosen option.
      • To deselect your answer, click the bubble again or click “Clear Response”.
      • To change your answer, click on another bubble.
      • Click “Save & Next” to save your response.
    2. Procedure for answering a Numerical Answer Type (NAT) question:
      • Use the virtual numerical keypad to enter your answer.
      • Decimals & negatives allowed (e.g. -.3, 12.5435, etc.).
      • To clear, click “Clear Response”.
      • To save, click “Save & Next”.
    3. Click “Mark for Review & Next” to mark a question for later review. Answered + marked for review will be counted in evaluation.
    4. To change a saved answer, go to the question again and re-answer.
    5. Only saved or marked-for-review questions will be evaluated.
    6. Sections are listed at the top. Click to switch sections. The current section is highlighted.
    7. After answering the last question in a section, you will automatically move to the next section.
    8. Hover on a section tab to view the answering summary.

SSC CHSL Tier-1 2024: Official Paper

Held On: 01 Jul, 2024 Shift 1

Maximum Marks: 200

Duration: 60 Mins

Read the following instructions carefully.

  1. The test contains 4 sections having 100 total questions.
  2. Each question has 4 options out of which only one is correct.
  3. You have to finish the test in 60 minutes.
  4. Try not to guess the answer as there is negative marking.
  5. You will be awarded 2 marks for each correct answer and 0.5 marks will be deducted for each wrong answer.
  6. There is no negative marking for the questions that you have not attempted.
  7. You can write this test only once. Make sure that you complete the test before you submit or close the browser.
