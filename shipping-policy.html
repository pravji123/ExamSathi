Shipping & Delivery Policy

This policy applies to all purchases made through Testara for digital content, including online mock tests, study materials, and other electronic resources.

Digital Products

Delivery method

  • For digital content, delivery is made electronically via your registered Testara account or the email address provided at checkout.
  • Access is typically granted instantly after successful payment, or within 1–2 business days if manual processing is required.
  • You will receive a confirmation email containing access details once your order is processed.

Physical Products (if applicable)

Shipping carriers & timelines

  • If your order includes any physical items (for example, printed materials), shipping will be arranged as follows:
  • International Buyers: Shipments are sent via registered international courier companies and/or International Speed Post.
  • Domestic Buyers: Shipments are sent via registered domestic courier companies and/or Speed Post.
  • Orders are dispatched within 1–2 days or according to the delivery date agreed at the time of order confirmation. Delivery is subject to the courier/postal service norms.
  • Testara is not responsible for delays caused by courier or postal authorities; our responsibility is to hand over the consignment to the chosen carrier within the committed dispatch timeframe.

General Terms

  • Delivery of all orders will be made to the email address (for digital products) or shipping address (for physical products) provided by the buyer at checkout.
  • For digital products, please ensure your email address is correct to avoid delivery issues.
  • If you face any issues in accessing your purchased content, please contact our helpdesk (details below).
